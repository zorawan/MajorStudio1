Velva E. Rudd

A specialist in tropical Leguminosae, began her career in the Botany Department as a technician (1948-1973). She was later promoted to Assistant Curator and eventually to Curator. Her extensive work on legume taxonomy is exemplified in a six-part monograph in Contributions from the United States National Herbarium (1955-1968).
